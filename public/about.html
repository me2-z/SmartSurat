About Smart Surat

Empowering citizens of Surat to participate in city governance through technology. Building a smarter, cleaner, and more responsive urban environment.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities, enabling efficient reporting and resolution of civic issues for a sustainable Surat.

Citizen Empowerment

We provide tools for residents to voice concerns and track progress, fostering community involvement in city development.

Efficient Governance

Authorities gain real-time insights to prioritize and resolve issues swiftly, improving service delivery across Surat.

About This Project

This Smart Surat City Portal is a demo application designed to simulate the complaint filing and tracking process for civic issues. It uses client-side storage (localStorage) to manage data for demonstration purposes only. In a real-world implementation, this would integrate with secure backend services, databases, and authentication systems provided by the Surat Municipal Corporation.

Demo Features

  • • Easy complaint submission with categories and photo uploads
  • • Real-time dashboard for tracking status
  • • Local data persistence for testing
  • • Responsive design for all devices

Limitations

  • • Data is not persisted across devices or browsers
  • • No real server integration or notifications
  • • Admin functionality is simulated (client-side only)
  • • For production, contact municipal authorities
